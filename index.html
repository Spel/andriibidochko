About

I'm the CEO/CTO of UBOS.tech — a platform that helps businesses become autonomous, AI-first organizations. With deep roots in AI research and software engineering, my focus is on making AI agent orchestration practical and accessible.

UBOS (Unified Business Operating System) is a leading-edge low-code/no-code application development platform. It streamlines the creation of AI-powered solutions by chaining multiple LLM or AI/ML API services into a single flow — removing the barriers to AI adoption for businesses of all sizes.

Based in the Amsterdam area with HQ in Lviv, Ukraine. Currently pursuing a PhD in Artificial Intelligence at Lviv Polytechnic National University.
